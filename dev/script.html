***DUALITY***

Base game includes filler not shown here.

Setting of each chapter

  • Chapter 1: The House (very short chapter)
  • Chapter 2: Bus & School (very short chapter)
  • Chapter 3: The Woods (short chapter)
  • Chapter 4: Pastel Garden
  • Chapter 5: Gordonwood Farm
  • Chapter 6: The Town
  • Chapters 7-9: Pastel Garden (again)

Chapter 1: "Rhetorical Soul"

You wake up in a strange room filled with alien-like creatures, "Fin's", who all tell you to gain true flesh.

They guide you to a pedestal where a giant purple hand offers to give you life in exchange for your soul.

Your only option is to accept.

You wake up again—this time as Steven, in your bedroom on a Thursday at exactly 12:00 noon.

You change clothes, smell pancakes, and hear your mom singing.

Your brother Johnny is watching TV and doesn't want to be bothered.

Choice: Eat breakfast or skip it. ("Choice matters.")

You find your sister playing outside, staring into the bushes.

For a split second, you see the same Fin alien before it disappears.

Your sister informs you the bus is late. You both wait on the bench until it arrives.

Chapter 2: "School House Rock"

On the bus, you can talk to:

  • Evan: Asks about your day, then rambles about politics.
  • Luke: Asks about your family, then asks if you have a girlfriend yet.
  • Marty: Asks if life matters, then spirals into a different topic.

Amber, the bully, enters the bus and threatens you:

“Beat the shit out of you if you don’t move over.”

Choice: Move or do nothing. (This choice matters.)

  • Do nothing: Amber slams your head to the ground and walks away.
  • Move over: Does nothing, game progresses.

At school, depending on your bus choice:

  • Violent path: Ms. Ferrethall sends you to the nurse first.
  • Peaceful path: Class begins normally.

The nurse patches you up and sends you back.

In class, you can:

  • Sleep: Dream of melting Fin creatures. Wake up to find a bag over your head- Amber did it again.
  • Learn: Montage of listening, writing, doing classwork.

The bell rings. You get your things, pick up your sister from her classroom, and wait for the bus to go home.
